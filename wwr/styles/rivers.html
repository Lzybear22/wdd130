Our Favorite Rivers

At Dry Oar, we offer unforgettable trips on some of the most scenic and thrilling rivers in the country. Explore options like the Salmon River, Colorado River, and Snake River, each offering unique views, rapids, and camping experiences.

  • Salmon River – Known as the River of No Return, this one’s great for beginners and families.
  • Colorado River – Ride through the Grand Canyon with epic rapids and amazing scenery.
  • Snake River – A classic for spotting wildlife and enjoying calm stretches between rapids.
